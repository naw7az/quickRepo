1. React Lifecycle methods are categorized in 4 groups:
   a. Mounting - Mounting means putting elements into the DOM.
   b. Updating
   c. Error Boundaries
   d. Unmounting

2. Some of the most Important Lifecycle methods are:
   a. constructor()
   b. render()
   c. componentDidMount()
   d. componentDidUpdate()
   e. componentWillUnmout()
